About

How PLUR VR all came about.

  • 2015

    The Beginning

    Members of the PLUR team meets at a hackathon in Los Angeles, CA. We were all strangers.

  • October 2015

    Venturing Off

    Together, we ventured off to start our own side project, Drew's List, under Andrew's lead. We eventually became Totem V.

  • January 2016

    We like to work hard.

    Members decided to take on PLUR VR as a second project under Steven's lead and under his own company, Blastzone Studios. No big deal!

  • May 2016

    Launching

    PLUR VR launches after months of hard work! Let's party!
